MOHANDAS KARAMCHAND GANDHI


Gandhiji is considered the Father of the Nation in India and is commonly called Bapu.

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi, was an Indian revolutionary, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule and later inspire movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.
